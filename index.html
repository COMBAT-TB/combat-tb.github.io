COMBAT-TB

COMBAT-TB is a SAMRC and NRF funded project undertaken by researchers and software developers at SANBI that provides tools for understanding M. tuberculosis. Our focus is on two related initiatives:

COMBAT-TB eXplorer, a comprehensive knowledgebase of the M. tuberculosis genome and associated information.

COMBAT-TB Workbench, a set of tools and workflows for M. tuberculosis sequence data analysis.

We practice open science and open source software development.
